Purpose of Board:
- Turn on power and turn off power (Up to 250VAC at 20A)
- Output 5V

Inputs:
- 2x 120 VAC
- GND
- Relay Control Signal (5V Logic)

Outputs:
- 4x GND Pin
- 4x 5 VDC Pin
- 2x 120 VAC
